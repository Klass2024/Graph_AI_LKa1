About Me

Dr. Lassané Kaboré (Pharm.D, MSc, MSPH, PhD) – With over 15 years at the intersection of public health research, policy and program delivery, I evaluate the real-world use, impact and cost-effectiveness of vaccines and infectious-disease interventions. I currently lead evaluation & learning at Gavi, the Vaccine Alliance in Geneva, Switzerland, guiding studies on malaria, Mpox, Ebola and other vaccines.

I hold a PharmD, two master's degrees in International Health and Epidemiology, and a PhD in Global Health. Bilingual in English and French, I have authored 20 peer-reviewed publications and am passionate about translating evidence into action.

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

This 8-week live online certification teaches no-code AI tools to automate data analysis, content creation and routine workflows. Through interactive workshops and a hands-on capstone, participants build AI solutions that boost individual and team productivity.

Key Outcomes

  • Automate time-consuming document, spreadsheet and data tasks
  • Organise knowledge and accelerate literature reviews with AI
  • Develop custom chatbots to support research and operations
  • Create professional visuals and presentations in minutes
  • Design and present a real-world AI solution in the capstone project

Modules

  1. Documents, Presentations & Websites – LLM fundamentals, content generation, no-code sites
  2. Automated Data Analysis – ChatGPT code interpreter, Julius, Windsurf, data visualisation
  3. GenAI for Research & Text Analysis – Elicit, LiteRev, summarisation, sentiment analysis
  4. Custom GPTs and Chatbots – RAG systems, Botpress workflows, API integration
  5. AI Workflows for Task Automation – Lindy automations, Make.com workflows, task orchestration
  6. GenAI for Communication & Outreach – Ideogram, InVideo AI, lead enrichment, scheduling
  7. Capstone Project & Showcase – Solution design, integration and peer feedback
